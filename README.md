# ParkLyst — Smart Parking Platform Business Plan

Marketing Management course project developing a full go-to-market strategy for ParkLyst, an AI-powered mobile parking application targeting Istanbul's urban congestion problem.

> **Portfolio note:** This was completed as an academic team project at Istanbul University. This repository presents a privacy-safe portfolio edition and does not claim sole authorship of the original research.

## Project snapshot

- **Course:** Pazarlama Yönetimi (Marketing Management) — Istanbul University
- **Deliverable:** End-to-end business and marketing plan (47 slides)
- **Industry:** Urban mobility / PropTech / Smart city
- **Target market:** Istanbul — individual drivers and corporate fleet users
- **Analysis tools:** SWOT, Porter's Five Forces, PEST, 7P Marketing Mix, Break-even Analysis

## Business concept

ParkLyst is a digital platform that lets drivers find, reserve, and pay for nearby parking spaces in real time. Powered by IoT sensors, AI-based routing, and dynamic pricing, it reduces urban parking search time, fuel consumption, and carbon emissions. The platform targets both individual users and institutional partners such as municipalities and private parking operators.

## Main findings

- Break-even is reachable at just **28% of daily capacity** (142 of 500 vehicles/day), indicating strong viability headroom.
- A **%70 profit margin** reflects high scalability of software-driven revenue relative to fixed infrastructure costs.
- Visit frequency and behavioral segmentation drive better targeting outcomes than income-based segmentation alone.
- Influencer-only campaigns were deprioritized in favor of niche creator partnerships with measurable outcomes.
- A differentiated marketing strategy was chosen over mass marketing to serve both individual and corporate user groups with tailored propositions.

## Strategic framework

- **Segmentation:** Geographic (Istanbul), demographic (mid-to-upper income, urban), behavioral (tech-savvy, time-sensitive), psychographic (eco-conscious)
- **Targeting:** Differentiated marketing — separate campaigns for individual drivers vs. corporate clients
- **Positioning:** Comparative positioning vs. basic parking apps; ParkLyst positioned as a full urban mobility solution, not just a space-finder
- **Competitive strategy:** Differentiation (AI routing, dynamic pricing), cost leadership (tax exemptions), geographic focus (high-density Istanbul zones first)

## Business recommendations

1. Lead with accessibility and transport integration in all communications.
2. Activate corporate partnerships early — B2B revenue stabilizes cash flow before consumer scale.
3. Leverage TÜBİTAK, KOSGEB and İTO Teknopark incentives to reduce effective capital cost.
4. Build loyalty through the referral system and a tiered rewards program before scaling paid acquisition.
5. Measure influencer ROI before expanding creator budgets; prioritize niche automotive and urban-lifestyle creators.
